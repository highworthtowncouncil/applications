Help & Guidance

Type a name in the search box to filter the list, then click an entry to highlight its location on the map. You can also click any marker on the map directly. Use the + and − buttons to zoom, and drag the map to pan. If you cannot find a grave or need assistance, please contact the Council office on 01793 762377 or admin@highworthtowncouncil.gov.uk.
